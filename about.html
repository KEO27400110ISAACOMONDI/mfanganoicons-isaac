Who Am I?

I'm Isaac, a student with a deep interest in web development and digital creativity. I started this portfolio to showcase my growth as a developer and to inspire others from my community on Mfangano Island to explore tech.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To build user-friendly websites that represent the beauty and potential of local communities.

Vision: A future where young people in rural areas can learn, build, and innovate using technology.

Skills & Tools

  • HTML & CSS
  • Responsive Web Design
  • Community Engagement through Tech

More About the Portfolio

This website was made to not only serve as a portfolio but also to be a platform for showcasing projects that reflect life, education, and opportunities on Mfangano Island. From fishing to education, these projects highlight what’s important in our community.
